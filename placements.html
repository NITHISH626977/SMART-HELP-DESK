Placement Tips

Pro Tips from Placement Cell

Resume Ready: Keep your resume updated with latest projects. Always have a PDF version ready to submit.
CGPA Matters: Most companies have a CGPA cutoff. Maintain it above 7.5 for maximum eligibility.
Practice DSA: Data Structures & Algorithms are tested in nearly every tech interview. Use LeetCode daily.
Mock Interviews: Attend the placement cell's weekly mock sessions every Friday at 3 PM in Seminar Hall B.
Register Early: Submit your resume to the placement cell at least 2 weeks before the drive date.
